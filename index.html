About Us

Educating today's youth for tomorrow's future.
Helping to make even the smallest of hurdles and goals, which to some may seem out of reach, obtainable.

Let's face it - Not all of us are destined for college.

But some of us have crafts and skills to build a prosperous, fulfilling life shared with our community to make this world a better place.

We all have a dream to become better - to be better to ourselves and to our crafts. These crafts were what this great nation was built on, where our God given talents are used to succeed. And those come in a variety of forms:

  • Electrician
  • Plumber
  • Cosmetologist
  • Barber
  • Carpenter

These are skilled trades. For many, it may be their future.

Jack of Hearts is organized to assist young men and women with the resources and opportunities to make these dreams a reality.

Frequently Asked Questions

Helping tomorrow's future is a neverending process. Here are a few answers to questions you may have.

  • Who do we help?

    We strive to assist young men and women of Bastrop County seeking assistance with pursuing their vocational education goals.

  • Is Jack of Hearts a non-profit organization?

    Indeed it is! Jack of Hearts is officially organized as a not-for-profit entity in the State of Texas and all donations and contributions are tax deductible.

  • Are your events open to the public?

    Our annual Scholarship Banquet and Rednecks & Rockers events are open to the general public. Please follow us on Facebook and here for the latest information.

  • How can I help?

    The easiest way to help is to simply attend one of our annual events and generously donate knowing you'll be helping to enable the future of our youth who otherwise would struggle to achieve their dreams.
